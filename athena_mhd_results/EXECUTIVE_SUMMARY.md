## The Bottom Line for Filament Spacing

**Isotropic magnetic pressure makes the problem worse, not better.**

- IM92 predicts λ_frag = 4.0W. Observations show 2.1W.
- Adding magnetic support: λ_frag,mag = 4W × c_eff/c_s ≈ **7–8W** (β=1 case)
- This moves the prediction **further from** the observed 2× value.

## What This Means

1. ✅ **Simple magnetic pressure is ruled out** as the explanation for the 2× spacing
2. ✅ **Strengthens the case for geometric explanations**: finite filament length, external pressure, junction-dominated fragmentation
3. 🔄 **Magnetic tension** (from helical/toroidal fields) could still explain 2× by suppressing long-wavelength modes — but this requires non-axial field geometry not yet simulated
4. 🔄 **β-variation runs** (planned) will test sensitivity to field strength
