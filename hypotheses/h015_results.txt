H015 Analysis Results
=====================
RAR Fit: slope=0.44, intercept=6.26, R2=0.765
Sample size: 135 galaxies

Correlations with RAR Residuals:
log_SB_disk: rho=-0.125, p=1.474e-01, n=135
log_Sigma_star: rho=-0.144, p=9.547e-02, n=135

Validation with GSWLC (actual SFR):
Proxy log_SB_disk vs logSFR: r=0.378, p=4.595e-01, n=6
Proxy log_Sigma_star vs logSFR: r=0.356, p=4.882e-01, n=6

Conclusion:
The hypothesis H015 (RAR scatter encodes unmeasured galaxy physics like SFR surface density) is not supported by the current analysis. Weak negative correlations were found between RAR residuals and SFR proxies (rho = -0.125 for log_SB_disk and rho = -0.144 for log_Sigma_star), but neither is statistically significant at the required p<0.01 threshold (p-values 0.147 and 0.095 respectively). Validation with actual SFR data from GSWLC cross-matched galaxies (n=6) showed moderate positive correlations between proxies and logSFR (r=0.356 to 0.378), but these are also not significant (p>0.45) due to the very small sample size. This suggests that surface brightness proxies may somewhat relate to SFR, but the data is insufficient to confirm a connection with RAR scatter. The RAR remains remarkably universal, with scatter not strongly tied to SFR proxies in this dataset.

Confidence Score: 0.3 (low)
- Statistical promise: limited by insignificant correlations and small validation sample.
- Impact if true: moderate, as it would add a physical dimension to RAR scatter, but current evidence is weak.

Methodological Lessons:
1. SFR proxies like SB_disk are imperfect and may not fully capture star formation surface density.
2. Validation dataset (GSWLC matched) is too small (n=6) to draw robust conclusions; larger cross-matched samples are needed.
3. Analysis reinforces the universality of the RAR—scatter remains uncorrelated with most measured properties even with new proxies.

Meta-Check on Hypothesis Strategy:
- Over-reliance on RAR residual correlations may be limiting discovery. Multiple cycles (H014, H015) have failed to find significant correlates for RAR scatter beyond data quality artifacts.
- Recommendation: Diversify focus to other anomaly types (e.g., cosmological connections, multi-scale outliers) or test alternative explanations for scatter (noise at low accelerations, halo properties).
